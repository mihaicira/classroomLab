Working together for a competitive and inclusive Europe

Classroom Laboratory, Project No 18-COP-0016, EEA Grants 2014-2021

Objective 1
   To enhance the quality of education and training in HEIs through exchange good practices, teaching and learning methods, and knowledge transfer;
Objective 2
   To strengthen the cooperation between education and the world of work by bringing together in the classroom and strengthen the collaboration between HEI’s, companies, industry and nongovernmental organizations which offers work integration services for vulnerable groups - Roma, persons with disabilities, migrants;
Objective 3
   To develop the educational offer of the WUT and NTNU with the Classroom Laboratory Joint Course, providing practical introduction to the real-life cases in which students develop interdisciplinary and transversal competences;
Objective 4
   To increase the learning mobility and develop internationalization.
